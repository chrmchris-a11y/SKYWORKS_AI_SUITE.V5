# Operational Safety Objectives (OSO) Detailed Rules Guide

## SORA Versions Comparison

| Feature | SORA 2.0 | SORA 2.5 |
|---------|----------|----------|
| Total OSOs | 24 | 17 |
| Robustness Levels | Optional | Not Required |
| Key Differences | More comprehensive | Simplified approach |

## OSO Rules Breakdown

### SORA 2.0 OSOs (24 Total)

#### OSO #10: Pre-Flight Preparation
- **SAIL Range:** 1-6
- **Evidence Required:**
  - Pre-flight checklist
  - Risk assessment document
- **Example:** Comprehensive mission planning review

#### OSO #11: Detect & Avoid
- **SAIL Range:** 2-6
- **Evidence Required:**
  - D&A system certification
  - Visual observer training logs
- **Example:** Active collision avoidance system deployment

### SORA 2.5 OSOs (17 Total)

#### OSO #11: Detect & Avoid (Simplified)
- **SAIL Range:** 2-5
- **Evidence Required:**
  - Collision avoidance strategy
  - D&A system documentation
- **Example:** Strategic avoidance protocol

#### OSO #17: Operational Volume
- **SAIL Range:** 1-4
- **Evidence Required:**
  - Operational volume map
  - Safety margin calculations
- **Example:** 3D flight envelope definition

## Compliance Checklist

### For SORA 2.0
1. Verify all 24 OSOs
2. Collect comprehensive evidence
3. Validate robustness levels
4. Perform detailed risk assessment

### For SORA 2.5
1. Focus on 17 critical OSOs
2. Streamline evidence collection
3. Simplify risk evaluation

## Best Practices
- Always specify SORA version
- Maintain detailed documentation
- Regular compliance reviews
- Adapt to version-specific requirements
