GRC 2.0 Credits (EASA AMC1 Article 11)

Ground Risk Class credits from EASA AMC1 Article 11 (SORA 2.0). Note: Different terminology and credit structure compared to SORA 2.5.

M1 - Strategic Mitigations (People at Risk)
Level Credit Description
None 0 No strategic mitigations
Low −1 Low-level strategic measures
Medium −1 Medium-level strategic measures
High −2 High-level strategic measures

ℹ️ Column-min clamp applied in calculator logic

M2 - Impact Reduction (Tactical Mitigations)
Level Credit Description
None 0 No impact reduction
Low −1 Low impact reduction (parachute, etc.)
High −2 High impact reduction (validated systems)
⚠️ NO MEDIUM level in SORA 2.0 (AMC1 Art.11)
M3 - Emergency Response Plan (ERP)
Level Credit Description
None +1 No ERP (penalty)
Adequate 0 Adequate ERP documented
Validated −1 Validated ERP with authorities

✅ Official terminology: None / Adequate / Validated (not Low/Medium/High)
