Strategic Mitigations & TMPR

Strategic mitigations reduce ground and air risk before flight. Tactical Mitigation Performance Requirements (TMPR) map to OSOs.

Strategic Mitigations (M1 in SORA 2.0, M1A/B/C in SORA 2.5)
  • Time Exposure Reduction: Limit flight duration over populated areas
  • Operational Restrictions: No-fly zones, altitude caps, speed limits
  • Ground Observer Deployment: Personnel monitoring operational area
  • Sheltering: Operations when people are indoors/shielded
  • Ground Clearance: Notifying/evacuating people from operational area
  • Population Density Selection: Operating in sparsely populated areas
Tactical Mitigation Performance Requirements (TMPR)

TMPRs define minimum performance standards for UAS systems and operations. Each TMPR maps to one or more Operational Safety Objectives (OSOs).

TMPR Categories

  • TMPR #1: UAS design & airworthiness (OSO #02, #04)
  • TMPR #2: Maintenance & continued airworthiness (OSO #03)
  • TMPR #3: Crew competency & training (OSO #05, #06)
  • TMPR #4: Procedures (normal & contingency) (OSO #07, #08)
  • TMPR #5: Detect & Avoid (DAA) (OSO #10)
  • TMPR #6: C2 Link robustness (OSO #11, #12)
  • TMPR #7: Automation & Human-Machine Interface (OSO #13)
  • TMPR #8: Human error mitigation (OSO #14)

SAIL → OSO Rigor Mapping

Higher SAIL (Specific Assurance and Integrity Levels) requires:

  • SAIL I–II: Low OSO rigor (equivalent to "adequate")
  • SAIL III–IV: Medium OSO rigor (design assurance)
  • SAIL V–VI: High OSO rigor (certification-equivalent)
